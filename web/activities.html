ACTIVITIES

Adventure Sarai Hotel Skardu is the place to start enjoying the many attractions available in the area. Karpocho Fort, Deosai National Park, Marsur Rock, Sarfaranga Desert, traces of Buddhist ancestors, unique monuments of Shigar and Khaplu are just a few of the attractions that are easily accessible to our guests. From here you can easily start trekking to the feet of the Karakoram giants or an expedition to one of the famous peaks dreamed of by the greatest mountain lovers. Our guests can also count on the possibility of organizing motorcycle or jeep trips.
